Wayfinders

Wayfinders are a great way to communicate to users where they are within a long page of sections. They can be used with any type of navigation (horizontal or vertical). Just use the class js_waypointNav for the navigation and js_waypoints for the container of sections (div's li's or dd's)

Here's a demo for side navigation with wayfinder. Try clicking on the nav and just scrolling.
